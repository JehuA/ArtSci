Student or Faculty?

The BMAV department is made up of students who are well versed in both the sciences and arts. Their primary role is to comprehend research material and visually translate the information. Prior to the symposium, a BMAV student will be paired with a Rowan University research faculty member and/or student.

The two parties will learn how to work together to develop effective visual elements. This service and collaboration is available for all interested labs. Due to the limited number of available BMAV students, requests will be taken on a first-come-first-serve basis.

If you are a student interested in presenting your research (lab-associated or independent) we ask you to fill out the following Google Form. Any potential questions (general or project-specific) can be added to the end of the form.

The deadline is March 25th 2022
