About Us

The ArtSci Symposium Series is a student-led collaborative event organized by the American Physician and Scientists Association (APSA) and the Biomedical Art and Visualization (BMAV) Department of Rowan University. The mission of the ArtSci Symposium Series is to build a bridge between the arts, sciences, and technology to make scientific research more accessible to the general public, regardless of scientific literacy. This series is dedicated to fostering active research, team creativity, and practical application.
