About Us

A collaboration between BMAV and APSA, ArtSci Symposium is an event that allows individuals from the Biological Science fields to share with their fellow students and mentors personal projects that exemplify their knowledge and their passions.

From research to art work, explore and discover what the students of Rowan have to offer to world of Biology.
